    Features

    Fingerprint activated

    You don't need to worry about keys ever again. The biometric sensor ensures that you are the only one to unlock the bike with a mere finger scan. Your thumb can lead you the Street Mode,while your middle-finger will activate the Power-mode.

    Driving modes

    Most EU countries consider two-wheeled electric vehicles as motocycles if they can coach a speed in excess of 25 km/h and have move than 2 50 W of power.The G12 has a special mode to comply with the regulators for road vehicles - which limits the speed and power. Still the top-speed is just a tap on the touch-screen away when you need it

    Be informed

    Speed consumption ideal and colculated range, temperatures and much more. Sophisticated electronics collect processes and display a large variety of data to the rider,which helps to planyour trip.

    Performance

    No need to pedal

    You can pedal, if you want, assist the motor or drive the G-12 like a motorcycle. It will reach 65 km/h without any assistance by the rider.

    Battery

    Battery-pack

    A state-of-the art battery-pack, designed and manufactured by Rimac Automobili. Advanced algoritms calcukate the remaining State of Change,range and many other factors. You can even watch how each cell is behaving in real-time on your screen. We have spent years on the development of this system in order to guarantee a long battery life

    Mechanical

    Rugged design

    We have not only focused on the electric part. All mechanical components are engineered for demanding life over many years

    Chassis & Suspension

    The chassis and rear are made out of high-grade 250Mc4 street. The tubesare CNC bent and cut while the street metal parts are CNC laser-cut. All parts are welded on a custom-made jig in order to assure quality.
